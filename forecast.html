Alewife Telemetry - Lake Ontario - Forecast Temperatures

Published

July 16, 2025

Purpose

This site is designed to provide quick updates on projected water temperatures from NOAA’s GLERL website for specific areas of Lake Ontario.

This site will automatically rebuild every 12 hr and will pull the latest projection.
