Alewife Telemetry - Lake Ontario - Nowcast Temperatures

Published

November 18, 2025

Purpose

This site is designed to provide quick updates on projected water temperatures given current conditions from NOAA’s GLERL website for specific areas of Lake Ontario.

This site will automatically rebuild every 12 hr and will pull the latest projection.
